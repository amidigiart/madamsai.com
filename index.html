💎 Career Question: How do I negotiate equity in my new role?

⚡ Key Framework:
  1. ✅ Know your market value — research comparable roles on Levels.fyi / Glassdoor
  2. ✅ Understand the equity type (ISO, NSO, RSU) and vesting schedule
  3. ✅ Negotiate on total comp, not just salary — equity is leverage
  4. ✅ Ask for acceleration clause if pre-IPO/acquisition
💡 Pro tip: Frame it as alignment — "I want skin in the game"
